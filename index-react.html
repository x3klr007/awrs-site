Advanced Methodology.

Autonomous Navigation

Mounted on a guided steel cable with a motorized hydraulic lift, AWRS navigates vertical well paths and pauses at predefined depth markers to isolate surface-accumulated contaminants.

X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF)

The rotating analytical module points an XRF laser beam against metal casings to check integrity, paired with high-definition cameras for ML-based crack and corrosion detection.

Environmental Endurance

Built with corrosion-resistant stainless steel. Rigorously lab-tested to withstand up to 60°C and 100% humidity environments without sensor drift or signal degradation.
